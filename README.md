# Online-Bank-Account-Fraud-Detection

Banking industry has been undergoing major digital switch overs to provide enhanced services to the consumers, application for opening a new account is one of them. However, due to a fast-paced advancement in technologies that come with their loopholes, fraudsters have been using sophisticated techniques to commit fraud making it difficult for traditional rule-based systems to detect and prevent fraudulent activities. The consumers on the other hand may suffer from identity theft, monetary losses in some cases, service dissatisfaction, and sentiment affliction in some cases.
